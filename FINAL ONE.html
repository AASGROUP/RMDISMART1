About RMDIS MART

Founded in 2020, RMDIS MART has been committed to providing high-quality, healthy food options to our customers. We believe in the power of nutritious food to transform lives.

Our products are carefully selected and many are locally sourced to ensure freshness and quality. From our famous chiki to our cold-pressed almond milk, we take pride in every item we offer.

Our mission is to make healthy eating delicious, convenient, and accessible to everyone.
